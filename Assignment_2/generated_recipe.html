Spicy Potato and Onion Wheat Breads

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water, as needed for dough

Instructions

Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter for a few seconds.

Add the finely chopped onion to the pan and sauté until they become translucent and golden brown.

In a large mixing bowl, combine the boiled and mashed potatoes, sautéed onions, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well until all ingredients are combined.

In another bowl, take the whole wheat flour and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Mix well and gradually add water to form a soft dough. Knead for about 5 minutes.

Divide the dough into 8 equal portions and shape them into thin balls. Take one ball and roll it out into a small circle using a rolling pin.

Place a generous amount of the potato-onion mixture in the center of the rolled-out dough, then fold the edges over to seal the filling inside. Roll it out gently again into a flatbread shape.

Heat a tawa or skillet over medium heat. Cook the rolled-out bread on the tawa for about 1-2 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked through. You can brush it with a little olive oil for extra flavor.

Repeat the process for the remaining dough balls and filling.

Serve the spicy potato and onion wheat breads hot with yogurt or your favorite chutney.
